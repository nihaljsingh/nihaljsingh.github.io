thinkFOUND, a furniture company based in east London, invited me to build signage for the South Asian music festival, Dialled In. The festival wanted 8 freestanding signs that would show festival-goers information on directions, assistance or the timings of the lineup. The signs were used in both the Manchester and London events in the summer of 2024.
