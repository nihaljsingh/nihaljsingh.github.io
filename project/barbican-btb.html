Beyond the Box invited me to contribute to the co-design process for the Barbican Centre’s Renewal Programme, which aimed to ensure young people’s perspectives shaped the future of the space.

From October 2024 to March, I took part in monthly sessions exploring how the Conservatory and Lakeside could better serve visitors while respecting the Barbican’s architectural heritage. The project aimed to restore and enhance the space, making it more inclusive, sustainable, and resilient for future generations.
