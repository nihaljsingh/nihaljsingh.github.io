I’m a carpenter and maker based in East London. I specialize in creating custom furniture, exhibition pieces, and functional artworks, blending craftsmanship with creativity.

Sustainability and community are at the heart of what I do. I focus on creating high-quality, functional pieces that not only serve a purpose but also make a positive impact on the spaces they’re in.

I’ve had the pleasure of collaborating with Bryan Kneale RA MBE, Barbican, Tim Norris, Piehouse Co-Op, Baraj Matthews, and Beyond The Box. Each project has been a chance to learn and grow, and I’m always looking for ways to develop further.

I’m open to commissions and collaborations—whether it’s something bespoke or a creative solution for your project. Feel free to get in touch through my contact page if you’d like to work together or chat more about what I do.
